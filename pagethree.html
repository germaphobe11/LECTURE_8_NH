ANSWER TO QUESTON:

Another way to organize elements on a page in HTML without using relative or absolute positioning is by using the float property. By applying the float property to an element, it can be positioned to the left or right of its container and the content will flow around it. This is commonly used for creating layouts with multiple columns or to position images alongside text. values.
